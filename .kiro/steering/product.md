# Product Overview

## Drama Video Processor (短剧视频批量处理工具)

A batch video processing tool designed for short drama content production workflows. Provides three core capabilities:

1. **Video Merging** - Combines multiple video segments and subtitle files into a single complete video with synchronized subtitles
2. **Audio Separation** - Removes background music while preserving dialogue using AI-powered audio separation (Spleeter)
3. **Video Transcoding** - Converts videos to multiple resolutions and formats for different platforms

## Key Features

- Concurrent processing with configurable worker threads
- Resume capability for interrupted operations
- Support for SRT and ASS subtitle formats
- Automatic subtitle timestamp adjustment
- Multiple resolution presets (1080p, 720p, 480p, etc.)
- Detailed processing reports and logging
- Flexible directory structure support (original/ subdirectory or direct structure)

## Target Users

Content creators and production teams working with short drama videos who need to efficiently process multiple episodes with consistent quality and formatting.
